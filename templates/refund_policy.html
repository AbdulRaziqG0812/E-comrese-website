Return & Refund Policy

At AREEZ FRAGRANCE, customer satisfaction is our top priority.

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 3 days of delivery for an exchange or refund.

Items must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging to qualify for a return.

Refunds will be processed once the returned product is received and inspected.

We reserve the right to decline returns for used or tampered products due to hygiene reasons.
